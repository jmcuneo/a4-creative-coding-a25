Basic Keyboard Web Application

Instructions:

Changing the octave: Click on the option for the desired octave in the octave box.

Changing the smoothness: Change the position of the slider in the smoothness box. The leftmost position is least smooth, and the rightmost position is most smooth. NOTE: On the most smooth setting certain notes may not be audible in lower octaves due to them being difficult to perceive.

Changing the volume: Change the position of the slider in the volume box. The leftmost position is mute, and the rightmost position is the max volume.

Playing the piano: Either click on the keys to play them or use the keybinds provided.
