How to play:

Use the arrow keys to move the box. The left/right arrow keys move the box sideways, while the up/down arrow keys move the box forward and backward

Additionally, the mouse can be used to play. Hold down the the left button on the mouse and drag forward, backward, left, and right to move the basket

Catching the balls will increase your score, while missing one will reset it! Try to get the highest score!

The sliders on the top right of the page can be used to adjust the games settings

1. basketSize will increase the size of the basket

2. speed will increase the speed of the falling objects and basket movement proportionally

3. basketColor will change the color of the basket

4. objectSize will change the size of the falling objects
