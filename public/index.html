Creative Coding — Canvas + Web Audio

How to use:

  • Click Start Audio once (browser policy) to enable sound.
  • Game of Life: Use sliders (speed, cell size, random seed density). Click “Randomize” or “Clear”. Press Play/Pause.
  • Instrument: Click keys on the Piano. Adjust Volume, Filter Cutoff/Q, Reverb Mix. (Visualizer bars show audio.)
  • Song: Click “Load Song” to play your local MP3 (e.g., Twenty One Pilots — Stressed Out).
