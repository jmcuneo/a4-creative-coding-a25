Audio Visualizer Controls

Instructions

  • Load Audio: Click "Load Audio File" to upload your music
  • Play Controls: Use Play/Pause buttons to control audio
  • Sensitivity: Adjusts how responsive the visualization is to audio
  • Particle Count: Changes the number of visual elements
  • Rotation Speed: Controls how fast the visualization rotates
  • Color Scheme: Click to change the primary color
  • Test Tone: Generates a test tone if you don't have audio files
  • Mouse: Move mouse to control camera angle
