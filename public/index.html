Audio Particles Visualization

Welcome! This is an interactive 3D audio-reactive particle system.

How to Use:

  • Upload Audio: Click "Choose File" to load your own music (MP3, WAV)
  • Play/Pause: Control audio playback with the button
  • Particle Count: Adjust how many particles appear (100-5000)
  • Movement Speed: Control how fast particles move
  • Audio Sensitivity: Change how much particles react to music
  • Color Scheme: Choose different color modes
  • Mouse: Drag to rotate the camera view

Note: For best experience, use a song with good bass and variety!
