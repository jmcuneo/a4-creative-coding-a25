Kaleidoscope Drawer — How to Use

Draw on the canvas with your mouse (or touch). Your strokes are mirrored into multiple radial segments to create kaleidoscopic patterns.

  • Use the Controls panel (top-left) to adjust: segments, stroke width, jitter, trail fade, color mode, hue speed.
  • Press C to clear the canvas.
  • Resize the window to change the drawing area.
