Mahoney's Game of Life

A play on Conway's Game of Life - Cellular Automata Simulation
Developed by Grace Mahoney
Generation: 0

Instructions

Use the Start, Stop, and Step buttons to control the simulation. Start begins the animation, Stop pauses it, and Step advances one generation.

The Randomize button fills the grid with a random pattern and automatically stops the simulation. The Clear button resets all cells to dead.

Adjust the simulation speed using the Speed slider. Lower values result in faster updates.

You can paint cells by clicking or dragging over the grid to toggle them alive or dead. Painting resets the generation counter.

The simulation follows custom rules:

  • Overcrowding: 8 neighbors → death
  • Loneliness: less than 2 neighbors → death
  • Hysteria: even neighbors → higher chance of death
  • Summoning: all 4 corner neighbors alive → birth
  • Random Revival: 2 neighbors → 10% chance of birth
  • Conga: 7 neighbors → death & rebirth as a neighboring cell
